## Episode 143

I spoke with [Iason Gabriel](https://www.iasongabriel.com/) about:

*   Value alignment

*   Technology and worldmaking

*   How AI systems affect individuals and the social world

* * *

Iason is a philosopher and Senior Staff Research Scientist at Google DeepMind. His work focuses on the ethics of artificial intelligence, including questions about [AI value alignment](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11023-020-09539-2), [distributive justice](https://direct.mit.edu/daed/article/151/2/218/110610/Toward-a-Theory-of-Justice-for-Artificial), [language ethics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2209.00731) and [human rights](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.02667).

You can find him on [his website](https://www.iasongabriel.com/) and [Twitter](https://x.com/IasonGabriel)/X.

Find me on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/spaniel_bashir) (or [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/daniel-bashir/) if you want…) for updates, and reach me at editor@thegradient.pub for feedback, ideas, guest suggestions.
