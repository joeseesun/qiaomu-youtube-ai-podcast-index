# What is The AI Show?

**The Artificial Intelligence Show** began in 2021 from a simple need: to make sense of everything happening in AI. Understanding what it is, what matters, and what’s next can be overwhelming. We do the hard part so you don’t have to.

The Artificial Intelligence Show is your weekly guide to making AI approachable and actionable so you and your business can grow smarter. Hosted by Paul Roetzer and Mike Kaput, the show mixes timely news, listener-driven Q&A, and deep-dive storytelling.
